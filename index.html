About Us

The ASHISH VIDHYALAY School is one of the most prestigious school in Sabarkantha, founded as the"Unnati Edu Trust" in 1983. It is now run by Dr.AbdulRehman.There are large buildings in the school compound. The heritage building is the main centre for administration. The Principal's office,Staff room,and administrative office are situated in it. In theASHISH VIDHYALAY ,Kesharpura , students get the education from 1st to 12th.
